- MR triage
- Pipeline recovery
- Release-governor handoff
- Why parser reliability and benchmark lift matter for dev automation

Routes:
- `/v1/runtime-brief`
- `/v1/runtime-scorecard`
- `/v1/developer-ops-pack`
- `/v1/workflow-runs`
- `/v1/benchmark-summary`
